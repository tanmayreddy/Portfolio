Project

EnTc B.Tech Student

The basic idea of this project is to design a product which beats the traditional invasive method of determining the blood sugar level of a person.
This product sends waves of approximate wavelength of 940nm through the bloodstream which gives the amount of glucose flowing in the bloodstream.
It can also help us determine whether a person is diabetic or not. The amount of glucose can be determined by studying the FFT of the received signal.
